Our Mission

Welcome to Quigs

Where tradition, quality, and community gather in every shell.

The Botanical Sanctuary

Quigs Kava Lounge is born out of a desire to create a true alternative social space. We believe in the power of botanical beverages to bring people together, spark deep conversations, and foster relaxation without the typical drawbacks of modern nightlife.

Our lounge provides a cozy environment where you can work during the day with high-speed Wi-Fi, decompress after a long shift, or share a traditionally prepared shell of kava with fellow patrons in the evening.

Traditional Kava Sourcing

We source our kava root directly from established farmers in the Pacific islands, including Fiji, Vanuatu, and Tonga. Each strain is selected for its distinct noble qualities and is traditionally kneaded in small batches to preserve its natural active elements, offering a clean, relaxing experience.

Lab-Tested Kratom Purity

Quality and transparency guide our selection. Our premium kratom is sourced from sustainable Southeast Asian farms where leaves are harvested at optimal maturity. Every batch undergoes comprehensive lab testing to ensure freedom from contaminants and to verify consistent active levels.

Whether you seek a daytime boost of focused energy or an evening of soothing tranquility, our staff can guide you to the perfect strain and preparation method tailored to your personal preferences.

An Inclusive Community

Quigs is more than a beverage bar. We are a collection of creatives, professionals, students, and neighbors. Our space is built on respect, inclusivity, and shared positive vibes. Stop by to read, connect, play a board game, or simply enjoy a quiet moment with a premium botanical tea.
